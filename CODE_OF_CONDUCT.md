## Our Pledge 
In the interest of fostering an open and welcoming environment, we as contributors and maintainers pledge to making participation in our project and our community a harassment-free experience for everyone.

## Our Standards 
We expect participants to use inclusive language, be respectful of differing viewpoints, and accept constructive criticism gracefully. We are here to build better, faster, and more durable software together.

## Enforcement 
Project maintainers have the right and responsibility to remove, edit, or reject comments, commits, code, wiki edits, issues, and other contributions that are not aligned to this Code of Conduct.
